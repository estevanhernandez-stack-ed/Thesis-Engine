## Search Query Templates

For any domain, use these query patterns in Stage 1:

```
site:arxiv.org [domain] 2025 2026
[domain] "new research" OR "emerging" 2025
[domain] "under-explored" OR "gap" thesis
[domain] disruption "what we got wrong"
[domain] industry report 2025 key findings
```
